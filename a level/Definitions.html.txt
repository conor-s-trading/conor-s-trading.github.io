<h1>A-Level Economics Key Definitions</h1>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Aggregate Demand (AD):</strong> The total demand for goods and services in an economy at a given overall price level and in a given period.</li>
    <li><strong>Aggregate Supply (AS):</strong> The total supply of goods and services that producers in an economy are willing to supply at a given overall price level in a given period.</li>
    <li><strong>Fiscal Policy:</strong> Government policy related to taxation and spending to influence the economy.</li>
    <li><strong>Monetary Policy:</strong> Central bank actions involving the management of interest rates and money supply to influence the economy.</li>
    <li><strong>Unemployment Rate:</strong> The percentage of the workforce that is unemployed and actively seeking work.</li>
</ul>
